lemony allspice
cumin potato salad

Makes 6-8

Prep|Cook

15|35

ingredients

  • 6 medium Potatoes
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. ground allspice
  • 1 tsp. ground white pepper
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 4 scallions, chopped
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • fresh flat leaf parsley

preparation

  1. Boil the potatoes in a large pot of salted water over medium to high heat, uncovered, for 20 minutes, or until tender
  2. Drain, peel the potatoes and cut them into small chunks while still warm. Put the potatoes in a big bowl
  3. While the potatoes are still warm, add the olive oil and lemon juice to the mixing bowl. Season the potatoes with the allspice, cumin, white pepper, and salt
  4. Add the scallions. Toss the potatoes gently. Top with eggs and garnish with parsley. Serve at room temperature
